Version 3.1 Features Manual

Your tracker is now equipped with automation and wealth-building algorithms. Here is how to use the new features:

📱 1. Install as a Phone App (PWA)

iOS: Open this site in Safari, tap the Share icon (square with arrow), and tap "Add to Home Screen".
Android: Open in Chrome, tap the 3-dot menu, and tap "Add to Home screen". It will now look and behave like a native app.

☀️ 2. Light / Dark Mode Toggle

Use the button in the top right corner next to "PDF Report" to instantly switch the app's entire theme. The app will permanently remember your preference the next time you log in.

⚡ 3. The Quick-Add Button

In the bottom right of the screen is a floating blue + button. Use this when you are on the go (like in a coffee shop) to instantly log an expense or income without digging through menus.

🧠 4. Smart Wizard Memory & Sub Hunter

When you click Smart Bank Import, the AI will scan your raw bank CSV. If it finds a merchant you have categorized before, it will auto-select the category for you! The app will also scan your history to alert you of Recurring Subscriptions right on the dashboard.

📈 5. Debt Forecaster

Go to the Credit Cards tab. When adding a card, enter your APR and your Planned Monthly Payment. The wallet card will calculate exactly how many months until you are debt-free and how much total interest you will pay.
