Version 3.0 Elite Features Manual

Your tracker is now equipped with automation and wealth-building algorithms. Here is how to use the new features:

📱 1. Install as a Phone App (PWA)

iOS: Open this site in Safari, tap the Share icon (square with arrow), and tap "Add to Home Screen".
Android: Open in Chrome, tap the 3-dot menu, and tap "Add to Home screen". It will now look and behave like a native app.

⚡ 2. The Quick-Add Button

In the bottom right of the screen is a floating blue + button. Use this when you are on the go (like in a coffee shop) to instantly log an expense or income without digging through menus.

🧠 3. Smart Wizard Memory

When you click Smart Bank Import on the Transactions tab, the AI will scan your raw bank CSV. If it finds a merchant you have categorized before (e.g., "TARGET" as Shopping), it will auto-select the category for you! The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

🔮 4. Subscription Hunter

The app constantly scans your last 90 days of transactions. If it detects identical amounts billed from the same merchant repeatedly, a Subscription Alert Box will appear at the top of your Dashboard to help you catch forgotten charges.

📈 5. Debt Forecaster

Go to the Credit Cards tab. When adding a card, enter your APR and your Planned Monthly Payment. The wallet card will now calculate exactly how many months until you are debt-free and how much total interest you will pay.

📊 6. The 50/30/20 Health Bar

On your Dashboard, you'll see a dynamic health bar. It grades your current month's spending against your income using the 50/30/20 rule. Needs (Bills/Needs) should be under 50%, Wants under 30%, and Savings over 20%.
