The Operating Manual

1. Master Bills & Universal Calendar

The Blueprint: Use the Bills & Calendar tab to input permanent recurring bills (Mortgage, Car, Insurance) and their specific due date (1-31). These show up as BLUE outlines on the calendar, serving as your expectation template.

The Receipt: Use the Transactions & Log tab to log actual payments or daily expenses (Starbucks, Target). These will overlay onto the calendar automatically as solid pills, confirming you actually spent the money.
